About Research

This poster presents a more systematic and data-driven decision system for selecting airports and routes within regional connectivity schemes. This work is part of the PhD thesis work "Essays on Air Transport Policy: Optimizing Network Design for Regional Connectivity" undertaken by Siddartha under the guidance of Prof.Yash Daultani. The thesis further extends the presented work by incorporating accessibility, connectivity and economic development.
